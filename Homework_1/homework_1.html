Week 1 of Kickstart Coding (1/28/19 - 2/2/19)

Survived week 1 of my coding journey at Kickstart Coding. During the first week, we learned some basic coding terminologies such as the difference between front-end and back-end technology and what full stack means. We were also taught the basics of HTML, CSS and Bootstrap and like every aspiring programmer that came before us, made our very own "Hello World" page, officially marking the beginning of our coding journey.

After surviving the hello world rite of passage, our first homework assignment was to create our very own personalized professional webpage (what you're reading right now!) applying what we learned from the first week.

For the second week, we will be learning about bash, git and Python. Stay tuned for more and feel free to follow this page if you would like to see my progress and learn more about future projects
